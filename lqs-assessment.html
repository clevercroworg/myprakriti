Domain 1: Diet & Nutrition

1. How many servings of vegetables and fruits do you consume daily?
2. How often do you consume ultra-processed foods? (fast food, packaged snacks, sweetened beverages)
3. How often do you eat home-cooked meals versus restaurant or takeaway food?
4. How many glasses of plain water do you drink daily? (exclude tea, coffee, juices)
5. How would you describe your meal timing and eating pattern throughout the day?

Domain 2: Physical Activity

1. How many days per week do you engage in at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity?
2. How long do you typically sit or remain sedentary without movement during a typical day?
3. Do you engage in any strength or resistance training?
4. How do you typically get around for short distances? (less than 1 km)
5. How would you describe your overall physical energy and stamina for daily tasks?

Domain 3: Sleep Quality

1. How many hours of sleep do you typically get on weeknights?
2. How often do you wake up during the night and have difficulty going back to sleep?
3. How often do you wake up in the morning feeling refreshed and rested?
4. What time do you typically go to bed on weeknights?
5. How often do you use screens within 30 minutes of going to bed?

Domain 4: Stress & Mental Well-being

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed, anxious, or unable to cope with your daily responsibilities?
2. Do you have any regular stress-management practices? (meditation, prayer, hobby)
3. How often does stress lead you to eat unhealthy foods or overeat?
4. How supported do you feel by family or friends in managing your health condition?
5. How often do you feel hopeful or positive about your ability to manage and improve your health?

Domain 5: Emotional Health

1. How would you describe your overall relationship with food and eating?
2. How often do you experience positive emotions — joy, contentment, gratitude — during your week?
3. Do you feel your chronic condition affects your self-image or confidence?
4. How often do you eat in response to emotions like boredom, loneliness, sadness, or frustration?
5. How motivated do you currently feel to make changes to your diet and lifestyle for your health?
